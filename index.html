    The Story and Development of FBAmultitool

    In 2017 FBAleadsUK founder Zak Furness launched RestrictionWizard the first of its kind deal analysis tool starting off as just a tool with Restriction checker, hazmat checker, seller data & an advanced FBA calculator, but since has come a long way. Zak originally developed the tool to help with his FBA business & many other Amazon sellers he knew in the community. They all had the same issues when running there Amazon businesses with no solutions on the market.

    In 2018 RestrictionWizard was re-branded to FBAmultitool. The beginning of what you know today. FBAmultitool is proudly developed by a small team of 2-3 members. And continues to grow in popularity throughout the Amazon community.
